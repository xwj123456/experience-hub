# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone.

Participants are expected to communicate constructively, critique ideas rather than people, respect privacy, and avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, or deliberately disruptive behavior. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when conduct is harmful to the community.

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's security-reporting channel. Reports will be handled as confidentially as reasonably possible. This policy applies in project spaces and when someone is officially representing the project.

This policy is inspired by the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
